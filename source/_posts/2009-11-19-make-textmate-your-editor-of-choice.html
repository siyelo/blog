--- layout: post title: "Make Textmate your $EDITOR of choice" date: 2009-11-19 08:46:59 comments: true categories: author: --- If you are a master of Vim and feel like a sea-change, or if you're just plain scared, you can set up Textmate as your default editor. 

1
2

export EDITOR='mate -w'
export GIT_EDITOR='mate -wl1'. #This instructs TextMate to open with the caret at line 1 rather than where it last was.

Whack this in your .profile (e.g. ~/.bash_profile), re-source it ( source ~/.bash_profile ), and off you go.
