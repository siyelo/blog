TL;DR: Lean From the Trenches, Schemaless SQL & Blowing in Nintendo Cartridges


    05 Oct 2012 By Siyelo

    We bring you the third installment of our ever engaging Dev chat session shares.

    Dalibor recommended that we all read ‘Lean from the Trenches: Managing Large-Scale Projects with Kanban’  by Henrik Kniberg.  It’s a great look at a practical implementation of Kanban.  We’ll definitely be trying this out with future projects.

    Peter was all about Schmaless SQL  this week. Will Leinweber gave a great presentation on the topic at this year’s MountainWest RubyConf.

    Codegram’s Lightning talk on Object Orientated Nirvana  got Sachin thinking about ways to ‘force’ yourself into achieving a better OO design. You might not agree with all the rules but it’s a good catalyst for discussion.

    Glenn’s been trying to get REE-1.8.7 working on Mountain Lion and he found this guide particularly helpful.

    Finally, we were debating that all important, utterly-mystifying question… ”Did Blowing into Nintendo Cartridges Really Help?”. What do you think?Have a great weekend!
